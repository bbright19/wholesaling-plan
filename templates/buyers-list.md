# Cash Buyers List & Buy-Box Tracker

> Goal: **10–20 active cash buyers BEFORE you lock a deal.** Know exactly what each one wants (their "buy box") so when a deal hits, you match it in minutes and dispo in days.
> Workflow: capture every buyer in the **Pipeline** table (quick scan), then fill a full **Buyer Profile** block for your serious ones. Re-touch each buyer ~monthly so the list stays warm.

## Key Questions to Ask Each Buyer
1. What areas / zip codes do you buy in?
2. Property type — single-family, townhome, multi?
3. What's your price range / max purchase price?
4. Min beds/baths and square footage?
5. How much rehab are you comfortable with — light, moderate, or full gut?
6. What finish level do you build to — rental-grade or retail?
7. Do you flip, buy-and-hold (BRRRR), or both?
8. **What's your rehab budget per sqft for [my area]?** *(this is the cheat-sheet gold)*
9. How fast can you close, and is it cash or hard money?
10. Can you send proof of funds? Want to be on my deal-alert list?

## Disposition Tip
When you get a deal under contract, **blast your matching buyers immediately** with: address, ARV, repair estimate, your assignment price, and access details. First serious buyer with **proof of funds + earnest money** wins. Match the deal to the buy boxes above so you only contact buyers who actually want it.
