## Key Questions to Ask Each Buyer
- What areas/zip codes do you buy in?
- What's your max purchase price?
- Do you flip, buy-and-hold (BRRRR), or both?
- How much rehab are you comfortable with?
- How fast can you close? Cash or hard money?
- Do you want to be on my deal alert list?

## Where to Find Cash Buyers
- County records — recent cash purchases (no mortgage recorded)
- BiggerPockets forums (DC/MD/VA)
- Facebook groups: "[City] Real Estate Investors"
- REI meetups (Meetup.com) — go to 2-3
- "We Buy Houses" ads on Craigslist / FB Marketplace / bandit signs

## Disposition Tip
When you get a deal under contract, blast your buyers list immediately with: address, ARV, repair estimate, your asking (assignment) price, and access details. First serious buyer with proof of funds + earnest money wins.
