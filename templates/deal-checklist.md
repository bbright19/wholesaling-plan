# Deal Checklist — Lead to Close

> Work a deal top to bottom. Don't skip the legal/title prep.

## 1. Lead Intake
- [ ] Logged in Lead Tracker with source
- [ ] Got seller motivation, timeline, condition, price expectation
- [ ] Pulled ownership info (confirm seller can actually sell)

## 2. Analyze
- [ ] Ran comps → estimated ARV
- [ ] Estimated repair costs
- [ ] Calculated MAO in Deal Calculator (70% rule)
- [ ] Confirmed there's a spread for your assignment fee

## 3. Offer & Contract
- [ ] Made offer at or below MAO
- [ ] Seller agreed → signed **assignable purchase agreement** (attorney-reviewed)
- [ ] Collected earnest money ($100-$500 typical for wholesale)
- [ ] Opened title with investor-friendly title company

## 4. Find the Buyer (Disposition)
- [ ] Blasted buyers list with deal details
- [ ] Buyer agreed → signed **assignment contract**
- [ ] Collected buyer's earnest money / proof of funds
- [ ] Confirmed buyer's close timeline matches purchase agreement

## 5. Close
- [ ] Title clear / no liens surprises
- [ ] Closing scheduled with title company
- [ ] Assignment fee confirmed on settlement statement
- [ ] Collect fee at the closing table
- [ ] Update Lead Tracker → "Assigned / Closed"

---

## Legal Checklist (DC / MD / VA)
- [ ] Researched wholesale laws in the specific jurisdiction
- [ ] Real estate attorney reviewed contracts ($200-300, worth it)
- [ ] Understand disclosure requirements
- [ ] Clear on wholesaling vs. acting as an unlicensed agent
- [ ] Investor-friendly title company identified
